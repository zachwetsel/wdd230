Overview

Title

Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

This site's purpose is to inform people on the many businesses and attractions around Idaho Falls, particularly those associated with the Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce. The site will also provide information on current members of the Chamber and the opportunity to become a member of the Chamber.

Audience

The average audience of this site is local business owners seeking to gain support or join the Chamber of Commerce and people looking to gain more information about the businesses and attractions within Idaho Falls.

Scenarios

What resources and support services are available to help me start or grow my business?

Where can I find a visitor's guide or directory of local businesses and services to help me plan a trip to Idaho Falls?

Where can I find information on the benefits of Chamber of Commerce membership?
